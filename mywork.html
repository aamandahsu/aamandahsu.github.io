fine arts.

“Drawing inspiration from the recent takedown of the Ringling Brothers, I sought to explore deception through the lens of a circus, from the fabricated smiles painted on clowns’ faces to the mistreatment that occurs behind the stage curtains. I strive to convey the feeling of uncertainty and betrayal when the true colors of something is revealed. It’s uncomfortable, unsettling, and uneasy. This series exposes the downfall of the Ringling Circus, among many other failed circuses. The glitz and glamour they advertise merely cover up the exploitation of animals and performers that constitute their business.”
